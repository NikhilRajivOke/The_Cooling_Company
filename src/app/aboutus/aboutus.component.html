About The Cooling Company

Unique HVAC Solutions is Pune based reputed Heating, Ventilation and Air conditioning (HVAC) company established in 2011. We provide complete solutions starting from Designing, Consultation, Supply, Installation, Testing & Maintenance. We have set up HVAC installation for Corporate offices, Information Technology (IT) Park, Institution, Hospitals, Commercial buildings, Residential buildings, Multiplexes, Shopping malls, Telecommunication facilities and a host of others in various parts country. We believe in utmost customer satisfaction, competent performance and reliability, these qualities has gained us good reputation in the entire market. We believe that every aspect of work, be it sales, service, maintenance or consulting should culminate to service to the end user.
